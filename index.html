Help Train Our Robot to Clean Up A Machine Shop

By playing this game you will help train our robot to clean up a machine shop by providing realistic demonstrations of how humans perform this task. You will see 5 bins on a cluttered floor with objects like tools and scrap. You will have 60 seconds to click and drag as many objects as possible into the appropriate bins and we will show your score afterward!
