Help Train a Robot to Clean Up

Beta Test (November 2019)

Please take 3-5 mins to play this easy online game where you drag and drop items into bins as fast as possible. Your motions will help us train a real robot to classify objects into categories (tools, scrap, etc). Each session lasts one minute and we'll show your score right after!

More information about the project and similar games.
